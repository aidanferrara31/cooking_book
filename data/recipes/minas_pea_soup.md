# Mina's Pea Soup

## Ingredients

1. 1/2 lb bacon, diced
2. 1 small onion, diced
3. 3 tbsp parsley
4. 3 tbsp oil or butter
5. 1 package frozen peas
6. 1 qt. chicken broth
7. 1 cup rice

## Directions

- Cook back
- Saute onion, in butter then add parsley
- Add bacon and peas, cook with water to cover
- cook for **5** minutes
- Add chiken broth and rice, bring to boil
- Cook for **15** minutes, done
