# Cast Iron Cornbread

## Ingredients

1. 1 cups yellow cornmeal
2. 1 cups all purpose flour
3. 2/3 cups granulated sugar
4. 1 tsp salt
5. 1 tbsp baking powder
6. 1 tps baking soda
7. 1 1/4 cups whole buttermilk
8. 2 large eggs
9. 6 tbsp unsalter butter, melted & divided

## Directions

- Preheat cast iron at **450°** for **10-15** minutes
- In a large bowl, whisk together dry ingredients
- In a medium bowl, combine wet ingredients
- Add the wet ingredients to the dry, combine 
- Bake at **450°** for **20-25** minutes
