# Warmed Cranberry Brie

**Makes**: 8 appetizer servings

## Ingredients

- 1 (16-ounce) round Brie
- 1 (16-ounce) can whole-berry cranberry sauce
- 1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons spiced rum*
- 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1/4 cup chopped pecans, toasted

## Instructions

1. **Trim** rind from top of Brie, leaving a 1/3-inch border on top. Place Brie on a baking sheet.
2. **Stir** together cranberry sauce and next 3 ingredients; spread mixture evenly over top of Brie. Sprinkle evenly with pecans.
3. **Bake** Brie at **500°F** for **5 minutes**. Serve with assorted crackers and apple and pear slices.

## Timing

- Prep: 10 min.
- Bake: 5 min.

## Note

\* 2 tablespoons orange juice may be substituted for spiced rum.
