# Nana's Snow Pudding

## Ingredients

1. 1 pkg. Knox Gelatin 
2. water
3. 2 egg whites
4. 1/2 cup sugar
5. Juice of 1 lemon

## Directions

- Put gelatin in a bowl with 1/4 cup of cold water, let sit for **2** minutes
- Add 1 cup boiling water with sugar and lemon, mix
- Refridgerate until thick (**1** hour)
- Beat egg whites and add to gelatin mixture, beat together

## Topping

- Beat 2 egg yolks with fork
- Add 2 tbsp sugar
- Add 1 1/4 cup milk
- pinch of salt
- 1/2 tsp vanilla
- cook until just under a boil, reduce
