# Pine Pie

## Dough

- 6 cups flour
- 1 pkg. yeast cake

### Dough directions

1. Dissolve yeast in 1 cup warm water and add to dry ingredients.
2. Combine dry ingredients.
3. Add enough water to make soft dough. Knead.
4. Cover and let rise overnight.

## Filling

- 1 stick pepperoni
- 1/4 lb. prosciuttini
- 1/2 lb. salami
- 1/2 lb. bologna ham
- 1 pkg. mozzarella
- 8 tbls. olive oil
- 5 whole eggs
- 3 tbls. grated cheese
