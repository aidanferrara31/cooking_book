# Irish Soda Bread

## Ingredients

1. 3 cups flour
2. 3 tsp baking powder
3. 1/2 tsp salt
4. 1/4 cup sugar
5. 1 cup raisins
6. 2 tbsp softened butter
7. 1/2 tbsp caraway seeds
8. 1 large egg (beaten slightly)
9. 1 1/2 cup milk

## Directions

- Mix dry ingredients
- crumble butter into dry mixture
- add milk and eggs slowly, mix weel
- stir in raisins and caraway seeds
- bake at **400°** for **45** minutes
