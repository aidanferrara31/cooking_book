# Lemon Squares

## Crust

- 1 cup flour
- 1/4 cup sugar
- 1/3 cup butter

### Crust instructions

1. Mix until crumbly.
2. Pat into 8×8 pan.
3. Bake at **350°** for **10–12 min.**

## Topping

- 2 eggs
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 3 tbls lemon juice
- 1/2 tsp grated lemon peel
- 2 tbls flour
- 1/2 tsp baking powder (B.P.)

### Topping instructions

1. Beat together eggs, sugar, lemon juice, and grated lemon peel for 8–10 minutes.
2. Stir together flour and baking powder; add to egg mixture and blend.
3. Pour over crust.
4. Bake at **350°** for **20–25 min** until light brown.
