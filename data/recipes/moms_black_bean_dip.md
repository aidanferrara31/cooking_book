# Mom's Black Bean Dip

## Ingredients

1. 1 plump tomato
2. 2 tbsp diced onion
3. 1 tbsp fresh cilantro
4. 2 cans black beans, drained & rinsed
5. 1 tbsp cumin
6. 2 tsp hot sauce
7. salt to taste

## Directions

- Place tomato, onion, and cilantro into food processor, until finely chopped
- Add beans, cumin, and hot sauce, pulse until smooth
- Add salt to taste
