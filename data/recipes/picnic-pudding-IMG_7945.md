# Picnic Pudding

## Layer 1 (Crust)

- 1 cup flour
- 1/2 cup walnuts
- 1/2 cup coconuts
- 1 cup sugar
- 1/2 cup butter

### Crust instructions

1. Finger mix and line an 8×8 or 9×12 pan.
2. Bake at **350°** for **15 minutes**.

## Layer 2

- 2 pkgs milk chocolate instant pudding
- 3 cups milk

### Layer 2 instructions

- Prepare pudding with milk; beat together and put on cooled crust.

## Layer 3

- 1 (3 oz) cream cheese
- 1 tsp. vanilla
- 1/3 cup sugar
- 1 cup cream (beat until stiff)

### Layer 3 instructions

1. Beat cream cheese, vanilla, and sugar.
2. Add cream; beat until stiff.
