# Anise Cookies

## Ingredients

1. 1 stick butter
2. 1 cup sugar
3. 3 eggs
4. 1/4 cup orange juice
5. 2 tsp anise extract
6. 3 tsp baking powder
7. 3 cups flour


## Directions

- Add ingredients in order, beating after each addition
- mold dough into spoonful sized \balls 
- bake at **375°** for **15** minutes
- Use butter frosting/vanilla flavour
