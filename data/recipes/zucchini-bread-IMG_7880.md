# Zucchini Bread

## Ingredients

- 2 cups sugar
- 1 cup oil
- 1 tsp. vanilla
- 3 eggs
- 2 cups shredded raw squash
- 1 cup chopped nuts
- 3 cups flour
- 1/4 tsp. baking powder (B.P.)
- 1 tsp. baking soda
- 1/2 tsp. salt
- 1 tsp. each cinnamon, ginger & cloves

## Instructions

- Bake **1 hour** at **325°F** in greased loaf pans.

## Yield

- Makes 2 loaves.
