# Mince Meat Cake

Bake at **350°** for **2 hours**.  
Grease and flour tube pan.

## Ingredients

- 2 1/2 cups flour
- 1 tsp. baking soda
- 1 lb. mince meat
- 2 eggs
- 1 can condensed milk
- 1 lb can diced fruit *(word in photo is faint; may need confirmation)*

## Instructions

1. Sift together flour and baking soda.
2. Add mince meat.
3. Add eggs.
4. Add condensed milk.
5. Bake at 350° for 2 hours.

## Note

- Check after 1 1/2 hour (maybe even less time).
