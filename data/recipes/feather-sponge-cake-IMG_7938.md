# Feather Sponge Cake

## Ingredients

- 6 egg yolks
- 1/2 cup cold water
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
- 1/2 teaspoon orange or lemon extract
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 1/2 cups sifted flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 6 egg whites
- 1/4 teaspoon cream of tartar

## Instructions

1. Beat egg yolks; add water and beat until thick.
2. Add sugar gradually.
3. Add vanilla and extract.
4. Sift flour with salt; fold into egg yolk mixture a little at a time.
5. Beat egg whites until stiff peaks form.
6. Fold egg whites into yolk mixture.
7. Bake in ungreased 10-inch tube pan in slow oven (**325°**) for **1 hour**.
8. Invert pan to cool.
