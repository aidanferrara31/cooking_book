# Thumbprint Cookies

## Ingredients

1. 1 cup unsalted butter
2. 1/3 cup sugar
3. 1/3 cup light brown sugar
4. 1 large egg yolk
5. 2/4 tsp vanilla
6. 2 1/4 cup flour
7. 2 tsp cornstarch
8. 1/2 tsp salt
9. 1/2 cup sugar for rolling (optional)
10. 1/3 cup jam or reserve (raspberry works best)


## Directions

- Beat butter until creamy in stand mixer
- Add sugar, brown sugar until well mixed
- Add egg yolk and vanilla, beat well
- In separate bowl, whisk flour, cornstarch, and salt
- On low speed, gradually add dry mix to mixer and mix until completely combined
- Scoop cookie dough into 1 tbsp sized balls, roll well
- roll on granulated sugar (optional)
- Freeze cookie dough balls for **30** minutes
- Bake chilled dough at **375°** for about **11** minutes or until edges just begin golden brown
