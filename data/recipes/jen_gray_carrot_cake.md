# Jen Gray's Carrot Cake

## Ingredients

1. 2 cups sugar
2. 2 cups flour
3. 2 tsp baking soda
4. 2 tsp cinnamon
5. 1/2 tsp allspice
6. 1 tsp salt
7. * 1 1/2 cup wesson oil
8. * 3 cups carrots, grated
9. 4 medium eggs
10. 1/2 cups pecans, chopped
11. 8 oz cream cheese
12. 1 stick butter
13. * 2 cups powdered sugar
14. 1 tsp vanilla
15. 1 cups pecans, chopped


## Directions

- Combine all dry ingredients and mix well, slowly add eggs and oil, mix well
- Add carrots and beat on medium speed for about 2 minutes
- Grease and flour a baking dish
- preheat oven for 10 min at **350°**, bake for about **20-25** minutes

### Filling
- beat cream cheese and butter until light and fluffy
- butter first for 5 min, cream cheese another 3 min
- add sugar gradually
- add vanilla and pecans, put between layers and on top
