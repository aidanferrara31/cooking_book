# Auntie Susan's Egg Casserole

## Ingredients
1. 2 Jimmy Dean Sausage Roll Packages
2. Dozen Eggs
3. Milk
4. Shredded Cheese
5. Pepperidge Farm Stuffing mix 
6. 2 tbsp melted butter

## Directions
- Scramble sausage in frying pan
- Scramble eggs and 1/4 cup of milk
- Place cooked sausage in 9 x 13 in pan
- Add egg mixture
- sprink enough cheese to cover eggs
- Add stuffing mix and 2 tbsps butter
- Bake at **350°** for **45-60** minutes
