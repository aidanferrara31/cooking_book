# Recipe for Angelnette(s)

**Add in order — beat after each addition**

1. 6 eggs
2. 10 tsp. baking powder
3. 1 1/4 cup confectioners’ sugar
4. Juice of orange + rind
5. 3/4 cup (veg. oil)
6. 1 tsp. vanilla
7. 5 cups flour

## Directions

- Drop by spoonful onto greased cookie sheet.
- Bake at **400°** for **6 to 8 min.**

## Note

- Almond-flavored frosting.
