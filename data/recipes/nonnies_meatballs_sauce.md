# Nonnie's Meatballs and Sauce

## Ingredients - Meatballs

1. 1 lb ground beef
2. 2-3 slices of stale bread
3. garlic cloves, minced
4. handful of chopped parsley
5. salt & pepper

## Directions - Meatballs

- Combine all ingredients into one bowl
- mold into balls
- broil on high for **9** minutes
- turn meatballs
- broil on low for **5** minutes


## Ingredients - Sauce

1. 4 cans crushed tomatoes
2. garlic, chopped
3. Handful of parsley
4. Basil
5. sugar, salt to taste

## Directions - Sauce

- On medium-high heat bring sauce to a light boil
- lower temp to simmer for 45 minutes - 3 hours (longer the better)
