# Blueberry Cake

## Ingredients

1. 1 1/2 cup flour
2. 1 cup sugar
3. 1 tsp baking powder
4. 2 eggs
5. 1 tsp vanilla
6. 1/3 cup milk
7. 1/2 cup butter
8. 1 pint blueberries


## Directions

- Mix all ingredients together except blueberries, beat for 5 minutes 
- Add blueberries gently
- grease a baking dish and bake at **350°** for **45-60** minutes
