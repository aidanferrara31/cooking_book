# Blueberry Scones


1. 2 3/4 cups flour
2. 1/2 cups sugar
3. 3/4 tsp salt
4. 1 tbsp baking powder
5. 1/2 cups butter **cold**
6. 6 oz blueberries
7. 2 large eggs
8. 2 tsp vanilla
9. 1/2 cups milk

## Directions

- Whisk together flour, salt, sugar, & baking powder in a large bowl
- Cut in the butter
- Add the blueberries and gently stir 
- In a small bowl, whisk together eggs, vanilla, and milk
- Add the wet ingredients to the dry, combine 
- Bake at **425°** for **15-18** minutes
