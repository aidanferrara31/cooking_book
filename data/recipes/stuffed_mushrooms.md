# Nancy's Stuffed Mushrooms

## Ingredients

1. 1 lb mushrooms
2. 1 clove garlic, minced
3. 1 cups bread crumbs
4. 3 tbsp parm. cheese
5. 1 small onion, minced
6. 1 tbsp dried parsely
7. 3 tbsp melted butter
8. 6 tbsp olive oil
9. salt & pepper


## Directions

- Wash & clean mushrooms, remove stems, mince
- in frying pan at low heat, mix together oil, mushroom stems, bread crumbs, garlic, cheese, onion, salt, pepper, parsley, and butter
- stuff mushroom caps
- bake at **400°** for **20** minutes
