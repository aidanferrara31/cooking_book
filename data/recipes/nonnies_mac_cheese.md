# Nonnie's Mac & Cheese

## Ingredients

1. 9 tbsp butter, melted
2. 1/2 cup + 1 tbsp flour
3. 6 cups milk
4. Cayenne red pepper and salt to taste
5. 1 lb maccaroni penne
6. 18 oz cheese - 1/2 cheddar, 1/2 moterray jack
7. 1/4 cup parm cheese


## Directions

- Make maccaroni pasta ~10 min
- Combine all ingredients and bake at **350°** for **45-60** min
