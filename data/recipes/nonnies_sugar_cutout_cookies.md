# Nonnie's Sugar Cut Out Cookies

## Ingredients for dough
1. 1 cup of butter
2. 1 pck. cream cheese
3. 1 1/2 cup sugar
4. 1 egg
5. 1 tsp vanilla
6. 1/2 tsp almond extract
7. 3 1/2 cup flour
8. 1 tsp baking powder

## Ingredients for frosting

1. 1/2 stick of butter
2. 4 cups powdered sugar
3. 2-4 tbsp milk

## Directions for dough

- Beat together butter, cream cheese, sugar, and egg at low speed
- Add in vanilla, almond extract, combine well
- Slowly beat in flour and baking powder
- Refridgerate until firm
- Roll out and cut into desired shapes 
- Bake at **375°** for **8-10** minutes


## Directions for frosting

- Mix together with stand mixer: butter, powdered sugar, and milk - adjust milk and sugar for desired thickness
