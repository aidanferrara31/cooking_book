# Part Potatoes

## Ingredients

1. 5 lbs potatoes
2. 1 cup sour cream
3. 1 1/2 sticks butter, unsalted
4. 1 1/2 package cream cheese
5. salt & pepper to taste
6. Paprika

## Directions

- Peel and boil potatoes until soft
- In stand mixer combine sour cream, butter, cream cheese until whipped
- Add boiled potatoes to stand mixer, salt and pepper to taste
- mix on low speed for ~ **10** minutes, until chunks are gone
- Transfer to 9x13 in baking dish
- Sprinkle paprika on top
- Bake at **350°** for **30** minutes or until warmed through
