# Nana's Apple Pie

## Ingredients

1. Pie crust
2. 1 cup sugar
3. 2 tbsp tapioca
4. Cinnamon 
5. Nutmeg
6. Butter
7. Milk
8. Apples

## Directions

- Peel and cut apples into small pieces, butter bottom of pie dish, then add pie crust
- Add half the apples, add sugar, 1/2 of the tapiocca, cinnamon, and nutmeg
- Add remaining apples, tapiocca, add extra butter on top
- Bake at **400°** for about **15** minutes, then **350°** for about **45** minutes
