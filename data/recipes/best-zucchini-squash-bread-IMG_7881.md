# Best Zucchini Squash Bread

## Ingredients

- 3 eggs
- 3 cup flour
- 3 tsp. cinn.
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1 tsp. baking powder (B.P.)
- 1 tsp. baking soda
- 1 1/2 cup sugar
- 2 cups shredded squash
- 1/2 cup chopped nuts
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 1 cup oil
- 3/4 tsp. nutmeg
- 3 tsp. vanilla

## Instructions

1. Beat eggs until foamy and stir in oil, sugar, squash and vanilla.
2. Add other ingredients.
3. Bake one hour.

## Yield

- Makes 1 large loaf or 2 small.
