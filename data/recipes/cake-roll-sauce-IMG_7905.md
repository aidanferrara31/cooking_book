# Sauce for Cake Roll

## Ingredients

- 3/5 cup corn syrup
- 1 1/4 cup brown sugar (sugar-packed)
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1/4 tsp. salt
- 1 (6 oz) can evaporated milk

## Instructions

1. Combine corn syrup, brown sugar, butter, and salt.
2. Bring to boil; cook until mixture thickens (heavy syrup is formed).
3. Cool.
4. Stir in evaporated milk.
5. Serve with cake roll.
